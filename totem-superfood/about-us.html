    Introduction – Who We Are

    From a Personal Mission to a Global Vision

    Although officially founded in 2025, our product’s story began in 2012 with one person’s dedication to health, nutrition, and energy-balanced living. Over the years, functional smoothie recipes were developed, enhanced with superfoods like spirulina, turmeric, maca, açaí, and chia — creating powerful blends full of vitamins and clean energy. By 2017, this evolved into a popular green smoothie bar, gaining local media attention and building a strong community of health-conscious consumers.

    The Innovation

    12 Years of Experience, One Revolutionary Product

    In 2024, we set out to bring these unique products to a global audience. After a Design Sprint, we embraced freeze-drying (lyophilization) — a method that preserves the nutritional value of ingredients through quick freezing and moisture removal. The result? A shelf-stable powder blend that transforms into a fresh smoothie with just water — as nutritious and delicious as if freshly made.
