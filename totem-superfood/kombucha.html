    Kombucha

    A legendary tea drink from the ancient Orient. SCOBY, a symbiotic culture of yeast and beneficial bacteria, ferments aromatic tea to create a delicious and naturally healing sparkling beverage. In ancient Japan, kombucha was called the “Tea of Immortality.” In Korea, it has been used since antiquity for rejuvenating beauty treatments, and in China, it was the drink of emperors.
